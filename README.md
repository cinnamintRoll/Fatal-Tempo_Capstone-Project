## **Game Overview**
**Fatal Tempo** is a rhythm-fueled VR exergame that mixes indie music with intense combat and parkour. Explore a vibrant, neon-soaked world, blasting enemies and jumping through obstacles in sync with the beat. The mission? Reclaim lost indie music from the clutches of *The Dissonance* by conquering rhythm-based challenges and unlocking new tracks from Melt Records’ artists.

---

## **Features**
- **Rhythm-Based Combat**: Hit enemies to the beat, earning perfect, great, or missed hits that impact your score and health.
- **Parkour Mechanics**: Dodge, jump, and twist through obstacles while staying on tempo with the music.
- **Character Progression**: Level up, unlock abilities, new songs, and challenges as you go.
- **Song Selection**: Play through a curated list of tracks from Melt Records’ indie artists.
- **Difficulty Options**: The game has two difficulty options for both newcomes and rhythm veterans.
- **Immersive VR**: Designed for Oculus Quest 2 and 3.

---

## **Gameplay Mechanics**
- **Action-Based Rhythm**: You’ll need to move, slice, dodge, and shoot, all in sync with the rhythm. Every action is tied to the beat.
- **Timing & Accuracy**: Nail your timing for perfect hits, and avoid missed beats to keep your health intact.
- **Health and Lives**: Start with three health points (represented by circles). Miss too many beats and you’ll lose health. Three hits, and it’s game over.
- **Score & Combos**: Rack up points with perfect hits and combo chains to unlock new levels and songs.
